# FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Do you accept walk-ins?
A: Yes! Walk-ins are always welcome. Appointments are recommended, especially on weekends and for lash or nail-art services, so you can guarantee your preferred time and technician.

Q: Do I need an appointment?
A: Not required, but it's the best way to avoid a wait and reserve time with a specialist. Book online via the Book Appointment page, or call (415) 823-2519.

Q: Do you offer hard gel or artificial nail extensions?
A: No — the salon does not offer hard gel or artificial nail extensions. Focus is on natural nail health: gel polish, gel overlays, manicures, pedicures, and nail art.

Q: Can you accommodate elderly clients?
A: Yes — the salon is wheelchair accessible with no steps, and technicians are experienced in gentle, patient care for elderly clients and those with mobility needs.

Q: What products do you use?
A: Organic, non-toxic, and cruelty-free products wherever possible — gentler on nails and skin, kinder to the environment, without compromising a luxurious finish.

Q: Can you remove gel polish from another salon?
A: Yes — gentle gel polish removal that protects the natural nail. Mention at check-in if arriving with existing gel polish so the visit can be timed properly.

Q: Do you repair a single broken or damaged nail?
A: Yes — the Nail Repair service fixes individual broken, split, or chipped nails. Just mention it on arrival. Starts at $5.
